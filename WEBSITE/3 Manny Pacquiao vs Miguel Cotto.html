#3: Manny Pacquiao vs Miguel Cotto

Justin Constance SD. Espejo

The Cotto Training Camp

Miguel Cotto prepares to go sparring in preparation for his fight with Manny Pacquiao


Having fought in the welterweight devision for the majority of his career, Miguel

Cotto felt right at home preparing for his title defense with the rising Filipino

star, Manny Pacquiao. For this training camp, Cotto trainied in the country in

Puerto Rico where he felt very much at home. In this training camp, they were

confident of Cotto's power and that if they could land power shots, Pacuiao wouldn't

be able to take it. Cotto claimed that he had faced opponents much bigger and had

more power than the Filipino and that he was able to put a stop to those opponents.

In sparring, Cotto faced opponents of the similar weight that had the same southpaw

stance as Manny to prepare him for their confrontation.

Miguel Cotto(blue gear) spars Vince "Cool" Phillips(red and yellow gear) to prepare for Manny Pacquiao


The Pacquiao Training Camp

Manny Pacquiao(left) trains his right hook with head coach Freddie Roach(right)


With a supertyphoon that just finished its onslaught on Philippine soil, Manny

Pacquiao had to be ready to bring his own onslaught against WBO welterweight title

holder, Miguel "Angel" Cotto. To prepare for this fight, Freddie Roach explained

that Manny must not get cornered on the ropes to prevent the slower and bulkier

Puerto Rican from landing his devastating power shots. Pacquiao and Roach worked on

moving in and out and bringing more aggressiveness to the fight while still not

being careless.
