#1: Manny Pacquiao vs. Antonio Margarito

Justin Constance SD. Espejo

The Margarito Training Camp

Antonio Margarito(right) hitting the pads with head coach, Robert Garcia(left).


With his boxing license stripped away for nearly a year due to hand wrap issues

in his previous fights, Margarito is seeking to beat number one contender, Manny

Pacquiao in the junior welterweight division. Robert Garcia explains that Margarito

is the biggest opponent that Pacquiao has fought and that the mexican warrior must

put his weight advantage to good use and bully the Filipino boxer. Margarito trained

by learning how to crowd and push his opponents against the ropes to land heavy

power shots which is exactly what he would do against Pacquiao. Reyhdration is also

key to winning this fight as Margarito's nutrition will be tested on how he will

gain back the essential water weight to add extra pounds after the weigh-in.

The two fighters, Manny pacquiao and Antonio Margarito train side by side with Margarito talking about the fight


The Pacquiao Training Camp

Manny Pacquiao(right) doing interval training and plyometrics with strength and conditioning coach, Alex Ariza(left)


After conquering the best in 7 different weight divisions, Manny Pacquiao has agreed

for a fight with natural middle-weight Antonio Margarito. The training camp knows

and understands that Margarito is a lot bigger than the Filipino boxer and that

he will have to be a perfect fighter that night. This is where Alex Ariza, Pacquao's

conditioning coach came in. They focused on strengthening Pacquiao's body to

withstand the heavy blows that were to come while honing his explosiveness using

plyometrics and interval training specific to pacquiao's body type.
